Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Routerino ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our service.

Information We Collect

1. Traceroute Data

When you use Routerino to trace a route to a destination, we collect:

  • Network route information (IP addresses, hops, latency)
  • Geolocation data associated with network nodes
  • ASN (Autonomous System Number) information
  • ISP and hostname data for network hops

2. Account Information

If you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address
  • Password (hashed securely)
  • Saved routes and traces
  • Collection and discovery data

3. Usage Data

We may collect:

  • IP address (for security and rate limiting)
  • Browser type and version
  • Pages visited and features used
  • Timestamp of activities

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the Routerino service
  • To generate network route visualizations and artwork
  • To track your discoveries and award badges
  • To display your routes in the public gallery (only if you choose to share)
  • To improve our service and user experience
  • To protect against abuse and ensure security

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption. Traceroute data and account information may be stored on cloud database services. We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information.

Cookies

We use cookies to maintain session state and remember your preferences. You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences.

Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services for:

  • Geolocation lookup (converting IP to location)
  • Map display (OpenStreetMap/Leaflet)
  • Payment processing (PayPal) - only if you donate

These services have their own privacy policies.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Opt out of public sharing features
  • Request correction of inaccurate data

Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

Children's Privacy

Routerino is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
